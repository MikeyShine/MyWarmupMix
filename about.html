Our story

We didn't build this site because it seemed like a good business idea. We built it because music has always been at the center of everything we do — and we wanted a way to share that with the world on our own terms.

We've spent years curating mixes, studying what makes a crowd move, what makes a cookout feel like a celebration, what makes a warm-up feel like a ritual. That experience lives in every mix we put out — and we believe you can feel the difference between something that was crafted with love and something that was just thrown together.

MyWarmupMix.com is our answer to the question: what if someone actually cared about the music they were playing? What if every mix felt intentional — built for a specific moment, a specific feeling, a specific vibe? That's what we're here to deliver.
